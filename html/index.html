The Miami Hadoop User Group, or Miami HUG, is a community of people interested in Hadoop and its related projects. All skill levels are welcome, from beginners to professionals. We strive to become the hub of information about Hadoop and Big Data in Miami.

Hadoop makes storing and processing large amounts of data very easy. It offers redundancy, fault-tolerance and scalability with minimal hassle. A diverse software ecosystem has grown around it to provide additional functionality such as SQL-like syntax, key-value storage, workflow management, in-memory processing, etc.
